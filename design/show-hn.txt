BODY:

I made a little soccer game where you coach a team by describing the tactics in
plain words, and the AI writes the bot code for you. The catch is the AI isn't
allowed to come up with the strategy, that part is yours. You submit your bot and
it plays a ladder against everyone else's.

You can watch any match in the browser, and matches are deterministic so you can
share a link to a specific game. I built the whole thing with Claude Code over a
few days, and it's running on one small server, so be gentle with it.

Would love to hear what tactics people try.

Play: https://claude-ball.fly.dev
Code: https://github.com/amitayk/claude-ball
