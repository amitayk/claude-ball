    Coaching tips

    Design in phases - decide which phase you're in, then give each player a job. You describe it; your assistant codes it.

    • Kickoff: play the ball back - pick the taker and a safe outlet.
    • In possession: the ball-carrier dribbles / passes / shoots; others spread out and offer lanes.
    • Out of possession: press or contain; mark their danger man, guard your goal.
    • Loose ball: decide who chases and who holds shape.
    • Shooting: only when it's on - close, good angle, clear lane.
    • Tune knobs, change one thing, commit. Roll back from Versions if it got worse.
